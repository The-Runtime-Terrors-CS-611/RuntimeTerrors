Rudy Interview #1

Interview Info

  • Date: 2/05/2026
  • Interviewer: Rudon Bastian
  • Duration: 30 Minutes

Top Takeaways

  • Needs low-friction, mobile-first gameplay that can be paused easily.
  • Motivated by personal agency and "gray area" morality rather than simple good vs. evil.
  • Values high-quality visual/audio immersion to escape a high-stress work/home environment.

Top Concerns/Turn-offs

  • Excessive "filler" text or long-winded descriptions that delay the next choice.
  • "False choices" where different options lead to the exact same dialogue/result.
  • Technical bugs or lack of an auto-save feature.

Top Desired Features

  • Branching narratives with permanent consequences.
  • High-quality art/soundscape for atmosphere.
  • Multiple save slots to explore different "what if" scenarios.

Notable Quote (Optional)

“Between keeping the network secure and keeping four boys from tearing the house down, I just want a game that respects my time and lets me be the hero—or the villain—for twenty minutes.”
