Matt Interview #1

Interview Info

  • Date: 1/29/2026
  • Interviewer: Matt McKay
  • Duration: 20 Minutes

Top Takeaways

  • She is mostly focused on the story aspect. Not a big gamer but loves fantasy novels. Interested in the worldbuilding.
  • To the same point, wants it to be longer than just an afternoon. She is interested in the story so ideally multiple hour long play sessions.
  • Wants choices to matter and have immediate payoff/repercussions

Top Concerns/Turn-offs

  • Needs to get into action fast, not a ton of lore/worldbuilding up front before choices take place
  • Choices not having immediate outcome in story, reaction from other character, etc. Choices need to matter.

Top Desired Features

  • Ability to replay chapters to change story without replaying whole game.
  • Flexible character morality that changes based on decisions made
  • Ability to save and come back later
