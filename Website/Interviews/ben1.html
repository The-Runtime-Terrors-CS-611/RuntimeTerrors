Ben Interview #1

Interview Info

  • Date: 1/28/2026
  • Interviewer: Ben Waddell
  • Duration: 45 Minutes

Top Takeaways

  • The biggest “hook” for them is an immersive, alive world where relationships change the story and affect future outcomes.
  • They want meaningful choices with real consequences, including compound/conditional outcomes (specific combinations of earlier decisions unlock different paths later).
  • They’re into low-fantasy, grounded, serious tone, but not “save the world” epic.
  • They like light systems layered onto narrative: resource tradeoffs (rations now vs buffs later) and tactical/task direction choices (assign positions in an encounter).

Top Concerns/Turn-offs

  • False choice / linearity: If choices don’t matter and it feels like a single track, they’ll bounce.
  • No save/checkpoint: If death or a branch ending forces restarting from the beginning, that’s a major turn-off.
  • They don’t want the game to lean into social/political commentary; specifically mentioned avoiding focus on sexuality/gender identity topics.

Top Desired Features

  • Save/Load with checkpoints (not on every screen, but reliable enough to avoid full restarts)
  • Relationship tracking / reputation scale that visibly shifts based on choices
  • More branching / replay value (more story branches; later updates could add multiclassing / deeper character customization)

Notable Quote (Optional)

“I like compounding decisions that a required to be made to acess a certain branch. As an example: I’m where I am in the story because I made choice A at story point 2 and choice C at story point 5.”
