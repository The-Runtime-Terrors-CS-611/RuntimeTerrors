Chris Soard, General Contractor

Chris is a mid-30s general contractor. He was born and raised in Indianapolis, and his parents are both accountants. Although he got his undergraduate degree in Architectural Design from the University of Cincinnati, the pandemic uprooted his post-grad plans. He moved back to Indy to be closer to his family and got a job with a local remodeling company. In his free time, Chris relaxes mainly by playing video games at home. His favorite genre is role playing games where your in-game decisions shape the world and the story. He usually plays on console.
