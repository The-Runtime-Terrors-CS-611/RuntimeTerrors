Matt Interview #2

Interview Info

  • Date: 1/30/2026
  • Interviewer: Matt McKay
  • Duration: 15 Minutes

Top Takeaways

  • He is a big gamer so wants more of the video game aspects
  • He is equally interested in how the decisions affect the main character as he is with the story/plot.
  • Wants to play on console, and total playtime could be like 4-5 hours. Short and sweet, easily replayable

Top Concerns/Turn-offs

  • A story that isn’t going anywhere, a lot of choices but nothing actually happening

Top Desired Features

  • Resource Allocation (like pack to hold things that could help in storyline, give other options)
  • Day Tracker/Journal that keeps track of what you’ve done that day
  • Time system/constraint. Like you have 7 days or whatever to save them or game over
